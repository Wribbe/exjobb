Test information

  • What - This test consists of abstracted representations of pseudo-managerial tasks, where the goal is to find a specific element as fast as possible.
  • Why - The goal is to examine how different combinations of text, color, contrast etc. impact reaction times of users.
  • How - The server will keep track of when the task was started. When the taks is finished, the final time and variables used to generate the test-data are recorded.

Data collection and publication

  • The goal is to record as little, ideally no, personally identifiable information about the participants of this test.
  • If identifiable patterns emerge they will not be included in any publicly distributed material.
  • Any statistical reporting will be done in aggregate and not focus on individual test-results.
